Snake

About

About

Snake is a 2-D game that runs on Java. The game is infinite and your score is based on the length of your snake. You can grow your snake by eating the red "food". The maximum length is 100. Snake is also available for 2 players. The person who lasts the longest or makes it to 100 first wins.
