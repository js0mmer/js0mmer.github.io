Featured

Block Runner

Block Runner is a 3-D game powered by the Unity Engine where you are controlling a block that has to dodge obstacles. The game is infinite and your score is based on how far you have traveled. There are three difficulties: easy, medium, and hard.

Snake

Snake is a 2-D game that runs on Java. The game is infinite and your score is based on the length of your snake. You can grow your snake by eating the red "food".

Hangman

Hangman is an HTML/JavaScript remake of the classic hangman. You have the option of playing against a bot with randomly generated words or with a friend who choses the words.
